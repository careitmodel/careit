# Definition

CARE-IT is a domain-specific reference model for the governance and operational management of clinically relevant digital clinical infrastructure within healthcare institutions.

It defines a structural framework that ensures digital decisions are consistently aligned with clinical effectiveness, while systematically addressing operation, risk, and responsibility allocation.

CARE-IT is:

- not a product  
- not a certification  
- not an IT service management framework  
- not a regulatory standard  

CARE-IT is a structuring leadership and governance model.

---

## Core Function

CARE-IT establishes a binding decision logic for digital systems that:

- are intended to support clinical effectiveness,
- may generate patient-relevant risks,
- require long-term operational sustainability,
- are embedded within complex clinical system constellations.

It ensures that digital infrastructure is governed not in technical isolation, but systemically and in alignment with clinical capability.

---

## Object of Application

CARE-IT does not evaluate individual software products or devices.

It evaluates digital clinical infrastructure as a clinical system constellation.

A clinical system constellation includes:

- clinical application systems,
- medical devices,
- interfaces,
- data flows,
- organizational responsibility allocation,
- operational processes.

The unit of evaluation is not the individual product, but the effectiveness-relevant overall system.

---

## Structural Components

CARE-IT consists of:

1. Eight foundational principles (normative guidelines)  
2. A two-level maturity model  
3. Six structural domains  
4. A defined artifact architecture  
5. An implementation and governance logic  

These components are structurally interconnected.

---

## Objective

CARE-IT aims to structurally enable organizations to:

- prioritize digital decisions according to clinical effectiveness,  
- assign responsibility explicitly,  
- manage risks consciously,  
- ensure long-term lifecycle sustainability,  
- integrate innovation within a defined normative boundary.  

CARE-IT does not replace existing models.  
It provides a connecting reference framework between clinical governance, technical implementation, and organizational oversight.

---

CARE-IT is a reference framework for structural decision capability in digital clinical operations.
