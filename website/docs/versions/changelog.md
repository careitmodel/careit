# Changelog

This document describes the versioned evolution of the CARE-IT Framework.

CARE-IT follows semantic versioning:

- **Major** – Structural redefinition or conceptual paradigm shift  
- **Minor** – Architectural extension, governance consolidation, or structural refinement  
- **Patch** – Editorial or formal adjustments without normative modification  

---

## Version 1.2.0

**Release Type:** Minor (governance consolidation and structural refinement)  
**Status:** Stable – English master version

### Governance Consolidation

- Introduction of a formal Framework Governance model  
- Clear separation between Framework Governance and Adoption Governance  
- Formal definition of English as the authoritative source language  
- Structured translation governance logic  
- Explicit articulation of the Controlled Open philosophy  

### Architectural Refinement

- Terminological harmonization across principles, domains, artifacts, and maturity model  
- Clarification of innovation from an operator perspective  
- Structural stabilization of governance language  
- Alignment of navigation structure with architectural logic  

### Internationalization

- Establishment of English as the normative master version  
- Alignment of translation structure with version governance  

No foundational principles were modified.  
The normative architecture remains stable.

## Version 1.1.0

**Release Type:** Minor (architectural consolidation)  
**Status:** Stable

### Architecture & Structure

- Full coherence review of all foundational principles (P1–P8)  
- Clear separation between principles, domains, and maturity model  
- Consolidation of the six architectural domains (D1–D6)  
- Integration of the visual overall architecture model  
- Clarification of architectural logic  

### Maturity Model

- Explicit rejection of point aggregation  
- Clarification of profile-based maturity logic  
- Separation between maturity assessment and KPI logic  
- Refinement of principle- and domain-based indicators  
- Structural derivation of the graphical maturity profile  

### Artifacts

- Harmonization of artifact structure  
- Clear differentiation between core artifacts and implementation artifacts  
- Refinement of the Clinical Risk Impact Check  
- Consolidation of artifact architecture as operational layer  

### Implementation

- Strategic revision of the Implementation Guide  
- Integration of governance and organizational aspects  
- Clearer derivation of development and prioritization paths  

### Consistency & Terminology

- Terminological harmonization  
- Sharpened guiding questions per principle  
- Clear normative positioning of P1 (Clinical Effectiveness) and P5 (Patient Safety as normative boundary)  
- Removal of redundant or overlapping formulations

# Version Governance

Each release represents a historically fixed architectural state.

Conceptual stability has priority over rapid expansion.

CARE-IT evolves deliberately through structured versioned releases.
