# Contact

For professional exchange, feedback, or structured input  
regarding the development of CARE-IT, you are welcome to get in touch.

General inquiries:  
info@careitmodel.org  

Conceptual contributions and structured feedback:  
contribute@careitmodel.org  

---

CARE-IT is an independent reference framework.

It does not constitute a commercial service,  
a consulting offering,  
or an advisory contract.

There is no entitlement to individual guidance or tailored implementation support.

Messages are reviewed and responded to  
based on professional relevance and alignment with the framework’s scope.
