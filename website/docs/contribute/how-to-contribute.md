# Contribute

CARE-IT is a publicly accessible reference framework.

Professional exchange and structured feedback from practice are explicitly welcome.

Contribution does not mean unrestricted modification.  
It means structured refinement within a coherent architectural framework.

---

## Forms of Contribution

Professionals from clinical practice, medical technology, IT, information security, enterprise architecture, and leadership are invited to contribute through:

- Professional feedback  
- Critical discussion of specific principles or artifacts  
- Identification of regulatory developments  
- Application-based experience reports  
- Structured proposals for conceptual refinement  

CARE-IT benefits from interdisciplinary perspective.

---

## Contribution Philosophy

CARE-IT follows a Controlled Open approach.

This means:

- The framework is openly accessible.
- Contributions are encouraged.
- Evolution is curated.
- Architectural coherence is preserved.

CARE-IT is not a wiki and not consensus-driven.

Conceptual integrity takes precedence over rapid expansion.

---

## Refinement Logic

CARE-IT is currently in a phase of conceptual consolidation.

During this phase:

- Clarifications are welcome.  
- Structural refinements are considered.  
- Terminological precision is encouraged.  

Core principles and architectural foundations remain stable.

All proposed refinements are evaluated in relation to:

- The eight foundational principles  
- The six domains  
- The maturity model  
- The artifact architecture  
- The governance structure  

Changes are incorporated exclusively through official version releases.

---

## How to Contribute

Contributions may be submitted through:

- GitHub issues or structured discussions  
- Direct professional exchange  
- Written conceptual feedback  

Each contribution should clearly indicate:

- The affected principle, domain, or artifact  
- The intended structural improvement  
- The rationale for the proposed refinement  

---

## Dialogue, Not Dilution

Digital clinical infrastructure is too complex to be shaped from a single discipline.

CARE-IT thrives on interdisciplinary dialogue.

At the same time, it maintains architectural clarity to avoid conceptual fragmentation.

Constructive discourse strengthens the framework.  
Unstructured modification weakens it.

---

# Closing

CARE-IT evolves deliberately.

Your professional insight can contribute to its refinement —  
while preserving the structural integrity that makes it reliable.
